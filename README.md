### Purpose & Objective
* **Objective:** To provide a dedicated digital gateway for pregnant mothers to access healthcare resources and register for antenatal care (ANC) services.
* **Problem Solved:** Mothers often lack a centralized platform to track health promotion activities or register for specific hospital services, leading to missed opportunities for vital healthcare support.

---

### Key Features
* **ANC Registration:** A specialized form designed to collect necessary maternal health data for hospital records.
* **Health Education Portal:** Centralized access to maternal health tips, prenatal care guides, and hospital announcements.
* **LINE Messaging Integration:** Built to work alongside LINE OA for automated notifications and easy access via mobile.
* **Optimized UX:** Clean, high-contrast, and easy-to-read interface designed specifically for accessibility.
